SLIDE 07

Low Intent Users: Problem & Hypothesis

Addressing the attendance rate and conversion challenge from increased bookings

The Problem (Post Nov 24)
1
Surge in Low Intent Users

New application made booking easier, bringing in users who "just want queries answered" without purchase intent.

2
Artificially Low Attendance Rates

5-10 min calls with low intent users weren't counted as "done" - marked absent, hurting attendance metrics.

3
Zero Conversions from Low Intent

Historical 0% purchase rate. No nurturing, no follow-up - just 2-3 such users per week, ignored.

The Hypothesis
1
Do Full Calls, Deliver Real Value

30-40 min value-driven calls. Complete GMAT/MBA roadmap, not just sales pitch. Build trust first.

2
Count as Attendance (Real Effort)

Mark full value calls as "done" - we're truly helping them, not dismissing them.

3
Proper Nurturing & Follow-up

Even 20-25% conversion from 0% = incremental purchases. Treat them as real prospects.

💡
Core Insight

Users came seeking knowledge, not a sales pitch. By providing genuine value upfront - complete journey roadmap, decision clarity, trust building - we hypothesized they would convert later. From 0% to any positive conversion = pure incremental gain.
